1. Scope of Work

Paul Walhus ("Provider") will deliver the following services to The Bergeron Group ("Client"):

A. Website Development

Provider will design, develop, and deploy a network of SEO-optimized real estate websites for the Client, each targeting specific real estate markets, neighborhoods, and buyer segments in the Hot Springs, Arkansas area.

  • Each website is a custom, mobile-responsive, SEO-optimized site with unique content
  • Each site includes Schema.org structured data, Open Graph tags, FAQ sections, and contact forms
  • All sites are cross-linked to form a strategic backlink network
  • Sites are deployed to agreed-upon hosting with custom domain configuration, SSL, and analytics

B. Podcast Studio Setup

Provider will design, configure, and set up a professional podcast studio at the Client's office, using equipment already in the Client's possession. Setup includes acoustic treatment, equipment configuration, recording software installation, and testing.

C. Ongoing Services (Monthly)

  • SEO & Content Retainer: Content updates, ranking optimization, Google Analytics/Search Console monitoring, cross-link maintenance, and monthly performance reporting
  • Podcast Production: Audio editing, show notes, graphics, and distribution for podcast episodes
  • AI Services: AI-generated content, avatar videos, market reports, and social media assets
  • Domain Management: DNS configuration, SSL certificates, domain renewals, and hosting maintenance
