How Lead Tracking Works

Every website I build has a tracking system that tells you exactly where each lead came from. Here's what it looks like in practice:

Example: A buyer searches Google for "Lake Hamilton homes for sale"

1. They find lakehamiltonhomesforsale.com (one of your niche sites)

2. They fill out the contact form on that site

3. You get an email that says:

Subject: New Lead from lakehamiltonhomesforsale.com
Name: John & Jane Smith
Email: john@email.com
Phone: (555) 123-4567
Interest: Buying a Home
Source: lakehamiltonhomesforsale.com
Message: "Looking for waterfront on Lake Hamilton, budget $350-450K"

You know instantly: who they are, what they want, and which website brought them to you.
