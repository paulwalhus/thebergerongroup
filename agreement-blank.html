3. Lead Generation & Performance Compensation

In addition to site development fees, Paul is compensated for the ongoing marketing value his websites generate for The Bergeron Group. This compensation recognizes that Paul's sites are purpose-built lead-generation assets that produce measurable business results.

3a. Lead Attribution

Every website in the network includes built-in lead source tracking. When a prospective buyer or seller submits a contact form, the system records:

  • Which website generated the lead (e.g., "hotspringsrvpark.com")
  • Date and time of submission
  • All contact information provided by the lead

A lead is considered "Paul-generated" when it originates from a contact form submission on any website built and maintained by Paul under this agreement. Leads arriving through other channels (CINC/Sync, direct referrals, open houses, sign calls, existing database contacts, social media, or paid advertising not managed by Paul) are excluded.

Why this matters: If someone fills out a form on hotspringsrvpark.com looking for an RV park property, there is zero ambiguity — that lead exists only because Paul built that niche site. The source tag is automatic, timestamped, and included in every lead notification email.

3b. Monthly Lead Report

Paul will provide a monthly lead attribution report showing:

  • Total leads per site
  • Lead contact details and inquiry type
  • Google Analytics traffic data per site
  • Keyword ranking progress

Nancy agrees to provide quarterly updates on which Paul-generated leads converted to active clients and which resulted in closed transactions, so that both parties can evaluate the network's ROI and prioritize future site development.

3c. Performance Compensation Structure

Paul's performance compensation is structured as a marketing services performance fee — not a real estate referral fee or commission split — and is paid in recognition of the digital marketing infrastructure that produced the lead.

TierTriggerCompensation
Qualified Lead Bonus A Paul-generated lead provides valid contact information and responds to initial outreach from The Bergeron Group $________ per qualified lead
Closed Transaction Bonus A Paul-generated lead results in a closed real estate transaction (purchase or sale) $________ per closed transaction

3d. Payment of Performance Compensation

  • Qualified Lead Bonuses are tallied monthly and included in the next monthly invoice
  • Closed Transaction Bonuses are payable within 14 days of closing
  • Nancy will notify Paul of any closed transaction involving a Paul-generated lead within 7 business days of closing

3e. Dispute Resolution

If there is a disagreement about whether a lead qualifies as "Paul-generated," both parties will review the lead source data together. The automated source tag from the contact form submission is the primary evidence. If the lead's first point of contact with The Bergeron Group was through a Paul-built website, it is Paul-generated — even if the lead later also contacts The Bergeron Group through other channels.

3f. 90-Day Review

Both parties agree to review the performance compensation structure after 90 days of the first sites going live. At that point, with real data on lead volume, lead quality, and conversion rates, both parties may renegotiate the per-lead and per-transaction amounts by mutual agreement. If no new terms are agreed upon, the original terms remain in effect.
