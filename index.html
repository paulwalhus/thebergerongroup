Why People Love Living in Hot Springs, Arkansas

Natural Beauty & Outdoor Living

Surrounded by the Ouachita Mountains and home to Hot Springs National Park, the area offers world-class hiking, fishing, boating, and scenery that draws visitors from around the globe — and convinces many of them to stay.

Affordable Cost of Living

Compared to major metro areas, Hot Springs offers significantly lower home prices, no state tax on Social Security, and a cost of living well below the national average — making it an ideal place to stretch your dollar.

Thriving Arts & Culture

From the Hot Springs Documentary Film Festival to a thriving gallery walk, live music scene, and craft breweries, downtown Hot Springs delivers a vibrant cultural experience with genuine small-town charm.

Entertainment & Recreation

Oaklawn Racing Casino Resort, Magic Springs Theme Park, championship golf, and two major lakes provide endless entertainment. Hot Springs attracts over 2 million visitors annually — and many become residents.

Top Retirement Destination

Consistently ranked among America's best places to retire, Hot Springs Village and the surrounding area offer healthcare, golf, lakes, low taxes, and a welcoming community of active retirees.

Strong Investment Potential

With booming tourism and growing demand for short-term vacation rentals, Hot Springs real estate offers excellent investment returns — especially near the lake, downtown, and Oaklawn.
