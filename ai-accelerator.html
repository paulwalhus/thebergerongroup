What to Tell Nancy About AI (Without Giving Away the Playbook)

Nancy already told you she loves tech and AI but doesn't have time for it. Here's how to position AI in conversation without revealing specifics:

Safe to say:

  • "I use advanced AI tools to build sites faster and better than traditional development"
  • "I'm part of a global community of 5,000 professionals who test and deploy new AI tools every day"
  • "As AI improves, your sites get better automatically — I keep everything updated"
  • "Your competitors are using basic AI. What I'm doing is several levels beyond that."

Do NOT say:

  • The names of specific tools (Claude Code, Stitch, etc.)
  • That sites can be built in hours instead of days
  • That AI writes the content (she might think it's low quality or that she could do it herself)
  • Anything that makes the work sound easy or automated — the value is in the strategy, not the typing
