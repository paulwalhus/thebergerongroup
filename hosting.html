The Solution: Modern Static Hosting

Your websites are built with clean, fast HTML/CSS/JS — no WordPress, no databases, no server maintenance. This means we can use modern static hosting platforms that are faster, more secure, and dramatically cheaper than traditional hosting.

Why Static Sites Win

  • Speed: Static sites load 3-10x faster than WordPress. Google rewards fast sites with higher rankings.
  • Security: No database = no SQL injection, no WordPress plugin vulnerabilities, no hacking targets. Your sites are virtually unhackable.
  • Uptime: Served from global CDN networks (300+ edge locations worldwide). 99.99% uptime guaranteed.
  • Zero maintenance: No server updates, no plugin patches, no PHP version conflicts. It just works.
  • Free HTTPS: Every site gets a free SSL certificate automatically. The padlock shows in every browser.

Domain Management Strategy

Current State

Your domains are currently spread across GoDaddy and Netfirms. This split makes management harder and more expensive. Here's what I recommend:

Option A: Consolidate to Cloudflare Registrar

  • Transfer all domains to Cloudflare Registrar
  • Cloudflare charges at-cost pricing — no markup, no renewal tricks
  • Typical .com renewal: $9.77/year (vs. GoDaddy's $20-22/year)
  • 50 domains: ~$489/year at Cloudflare vs. ~$1,000+/year at GoDaddy
  • DNS management integrated with hosting — everything in one dashboard

Option B: Keep GoDaddy, Use Cloudflare DNS Only

  • Keep domains registered at GoDaddy (no transfer hassle)
  • Point nameservers to Cloudflare for free DNS management
  • Still get Cloudflare's CDN, security, and performance benefits
  • Higher annual renewal costs but simpler transition

Either way, step one is the same: Export your complete domain list from GoDaddy as a CSV file. This gives us the full picture — every domain, every expiration date, every DNS setting — so we can build a migration plan with zero downtime.
