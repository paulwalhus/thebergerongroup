1. What This Agreement Covers

Paul Walhus ("Paul") will build a network of professional, SEO-optimized real estate websites for The Bergeron Group ("Nancy"), each targeting specific markets, neighborhoods, and buyer types in the Hot Springs, Arkansas area.

Each website includes:

  • Custom design with Bergeron Group and Keller Williams branding
  • Mobile-responsive layout that works on all devices
  • SEO optimization — Schema.org data, meta tags, FAQ sections
  • Contact form with lead source tracking (you'll know which site each lead came from)
  • Cross-linking to the hub site and other sites in the network
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Custom domain configuration with SSL
